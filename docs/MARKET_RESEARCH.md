# DataPulse - Market Research

## Executive Summary

DataPulse is an AI-powered business analytics platform that enables companies of all sizes to track KPIs, visualize data trends, and receive actionable insights. The platform targets the $30B+ business intelligence market with a modern, developer-friendly approach.

## Market Opportunity

### Total Addressable Market (TAM)
- Global Business Intelligence Market: **$33.3B** (2025), projected **$54.9B** by 2030
- CAGR: **10.6%** (2025-2030)
- Source: Fortune Business Insights, MarketsandMarkets

### Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM)
- Cloud-based BI & Analytics: **$15.2B** (2025)
- Self-service analytics platforms: **$8.4B**

### Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM)
- SMB analytics tools: **$2.1B**
- Target: 0.5% market share in Year 3 = **$10.5M ARR**

## Target Customer Segments

### Primary: Growth-Stage Startups (Series A-C)
- 50-500 employees
- Need analytics but can't afford enterprise tools
- Value developer experience and quick setup
- **Willingness to pay: $500-$5,000/mo**

### Secondary: Small-Medium Businesses
- 10-200 employees
- Non-technical users who need self-service analytics
- Value simplicity and guided insights
- **Willingness to pay: $99-$999/mo**

### Tertiary: Enterprise Teams
- Individual teams within large organizations
- Need rapid deployment without IT approval
- Value modern UX and API-first design
- **Willingness to pay: $2,000-$25,000/mo**

## Unique Value Proposition

1. **AI-Native Analytics**: Not just dashboards - AI automatically surfaces insights
2. **5-Minute Setup**: Connect data sources and get insights immediately
3. **Developer-First API**: Full REST API for custom integrations
4. **Transparent Pricing**: Simple per-seat pricing, no hidden costs
5. **Real-Time Processing**: Sub-second query performance on streaming data
